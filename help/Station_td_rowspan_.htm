rowspan Station td attribute
The row-span (how many units of the table grid a cell covers).
Sample:
<table>
   <tr>
      <th rowspan="2">Hello world</td>
      <td>Hello</td>
   </tr>
   <tr>
      <td>World</td>
   </tr>
</table>
